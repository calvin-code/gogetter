About Our Academy

The Go Getter Academy - Kyaani is a privately owned mixed day and boarding school situated at Kyaani shopping centre off MSA - NAIROBI Highway 7 Kms from Machinery (ZION JUNCTION) towards Kyulu hills. Discover our mission, vision, and values that drive our educational excellence.

Our Mission

To tap and nurture the talent of every child in preparation for holistic excellence in life.

Our Vision

Going for excellence through the acquisition of knowledge, skills and attitude.

Our Motto

"GO FOR EXCELLENCE" - This guiding principle inspires our students to pursue their highest potential while developing empathy and social awareness.
