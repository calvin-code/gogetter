The Go Getter Academy - Kyaani is a privately owned mixed day and boarding school situated at Kyaani shopping center off MSA - NAIROBI Highway, 7 Kms from Machinery (ZION JUNCTION) towards Kyulu hills. Founded with a vision to revolutionize education, we are committed to providing a holistic learning experience that goes beyond textbooks.

We offer a well-structured academic program that caters to students from early childhood through junior secondary school. Our innovative approach combines academic excellence with character building to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow. We believe every child is unique, and our tailored educational programs are designed to help each student discover and maximize their potential in a supportive and stimulating environment.
