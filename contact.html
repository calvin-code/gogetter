Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us

How can I schedule a campus tour?

To schedule a campus tour, please call our admissions office at +254 726 461 738 / +254 715 883 721 during office hours or fill out the contact form with your preferred date and time. We offer tours Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

What is the best time to contact the school?

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The best time to reach us by phone is between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM on weekdays. For non-urgent matters, you can email us at info@gogetteracademy.edu anytime.

How long does it take to receive a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. During peak admission seasons, response times may be slightly longer. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Can I meet with a specific teacher or department?

Yes, we can arrange meetings with teachers or department heads. Please specify who you'd like to meet in your message, and we'll coordinate with them to find a suitable time.

Do you have an admissions email?

For admissions-related questions, please contact adm+254 726 461 738 / +254 715 883 721. This includes inquiries about application procedures, required documents, and admission timelines.
