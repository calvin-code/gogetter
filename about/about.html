About Our Academy

The Go Getter Academy - Kyaani is a privately owned mixed day and boarding school situated at Kyaani shopping center off MSA - NAIROBI Highway, 7 Kms from Machinery (ZION JUNCTION) towards Kyulu hills. Discover our mission, vision, and values that drive our educational excellence.

Our Mission

To tap and nurture the talent of every child in preparation for holistic excellence in life.

Our Vision

Going for excellence through the acquisition of knowledge, skills and attitude.

Our Motto

"GO FOR EXCELLENCE" - This guiding principle inspires our students to pursue their highest potential while developing empathy and social awareness.

Our Core Values

The foundation of our educational philosophy at The Go Getter Academy - Kyaani

Our Values in Action

Students demonstrating our core values in daily activities

Value-Based Education

Creating an environment that fosters growth and integrity

Punctuality

We value time management and respect for others' time through promptness in all activities.

Professionalism

We conduct ourselves with competence, integrity, and respect in all academic endeavors.

Self Drive

We cultivate intrinsic motivation and personal initiative to achieve excellence.

Integrity

We uphold strong moral principles through honesty and ethical behavior.

Discipline

We maintain focus and commitment to achieve both personal and academic goals.

Hard Work

We embrace diligence and perseverance as essential pathways to success.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all academic and personal pursuits.
